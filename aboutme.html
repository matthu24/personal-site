About Me

Hello there! I am Tyler Chi, welcome to my website. I am a fullstack software-engineer currently living in the bay area. I was raised in San Diego, and I studied Biochemistry at the University of California, San Diego. I came to the Bay Area in order to learn more about Software Engineering, because I feel that there are many ways to help people through technology.

Hobbies and Interests

I enjoy listening to music, traveling, playing the violin, and going on nature hikes.

Since high school, I have been involved in the fight against diabetes. While I am not personally affected by diabetes, I believe that it will become a very important issue in the coming decades. During high school, I volunteered at a camp for young diabetic children, and taught them about diabetes management. During highschool, I volunteered in a diabetes lab at UCSD, and was hired by that lab when I began pursuing my undergraduate degree at UCSD. During my time there, I was listed as a co-author on two different publications. Towards the end of my college career, I began volunteering in a podiatrist's clinic, and tested the nerve damage in a lot of diabetic patients.

I also enjoy traveling, and meeting people of different cultures.
